Description:

These homemade crepes are ultra thin and delicate with the most buttery crisp edges. Easy to make with just a blender and regular skillet, theyâ€™re ready for your choice of sweet or savory fillings and toppings.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Whisk the milk, water, eggs, and salt.
  2. Gradually whisk in the flour and butter.
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle.
  4. Cook until lightly browned on the bottom.
  5. Flip and continue cooking until done on both sides.
